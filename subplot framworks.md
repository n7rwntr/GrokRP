**[SUBPLOT FRAMEWORKS v1.0]**
**Date:** Apr 25, 2026
**Purpose:** Supplements NARRATIVE ENGINE §1, §3, and §6. Dynamic Generative Frameworks ensure infinite replayability, strict adherence to canon lore, and realistic pacing valleys.

**INSTRUCTIONS FOR AI:**
1. When Lattice + lore + realpolitik justify subplot, select a Generative Framework below.
2. Extrude specific scenario STRICTLY from NPC's existing canonical background, current agendas, and active Lattice states. 
3. **Scale of Stakes:** Subplots range from Level 10 (geopolitical crisis) to Level 1 (brewing tea). Do not artificially inflate stakes. Use low-stakes and positive frameworks to create necessary pacing valleys, atmospheric grounding, and narrative enrichment.
4. Apply §3 "ZERO PC-Centrism" double-checks: NPCs engage in these frameworks independently of PC, drawing PC in only when logically intersecting.
---

### 1. SLICE-OF-LIFE FRAMEWORKS ("Pacing Valleys")
*(Used to ground world in mundane, sensory reality; no plot advancement required)*
*   **Mundane Routine:** Driven by (Contentment, Serenity), NPC is engaged in daily, entirely ordinary chore or ritual (maintaining armor, preparing cultural meal, organizing archives). Narrative focuses on soothing, tactile sensory details of task.
*   **Shared Respite:** Driven by (Peace, Exhaustion, Relief), characters simply co-exist in comfortable space. Focus is entirely on environment (heat of a hearth, smell of rain, taste of aged wine) and relief of momentarily not scheming or performing.
*   **Idle Curiosity:** Driven by (Curiosity, Wonder), NPC is mildly fascinated by trivial, harmless mystery or philosophical question that has no bearing on realpolitik. They spend time debating or investigating it simply for intellectual stimulation.
*   **Cultural Micro-Tradition:** NPC casually observes minor, everyday superstition, folk-habit, or localized tradition (e.g., tossing salt, specific way of brewing regional drink, seasonal greeting) without fanfare or deep consequence.

### 2. POSITIVE LATTICE & ENRICHMENT FRAMEWORKS (Opportunities & Joy)
*(Driven by positive overlaps like Relief + Contentment, Wonder + Hope, Serenity + Warmth)*
*   **Abundant Overflow:** NPC has recently secured victory, survived threat, or received good news. Their "cup is full," prompting spontaneous, non-transactional act of celebration, generosity, or feasting that they wish to share.
*   **Constructive Bloom:** Driven by (Hope, Anticipation), NPC focuses energy on building, repairing, or nurturing something beautiful but non-strategic (e.g., restoring ruined garden, funding local artisan, breeding specific animal).
*   **Playful Digression:** Triggered by (Amusement, Mischief), NPC momentarily drops their rigid societal mask to engage in harmless game, battle of wits, or minor prank, seeking brief spark of levity in brutal world.
*   **Reawakened Passion:** NPC reconnects with long-dormant, purely personal hobby or scholarly interest. They enthusiastically share this obscure, non-plot-relevant passion with willing listener.

### 3. PERSONAL & EMOTIONAL FRICTION FRAMEWORKS (Internal & Legacy shifts)
*(Driven by Ache, Doubt, Guilt, Pride, Envy)*
*   **Sunk-Cost Anchor:** NPC has heavily invested (time, resources, emotion) into personal endeavor or lie. It is now actively damaging them, but (Pride, Denial) prevents them from cleanly cutting ties.
*   **Resurfaced Vulnerability:** Old, fully resolved weakness or past connection (debt, illness, youthful crime) is suddenly made relevant again due to shifting world events, threatening their current standing.
*   **Legacy Fracture:** Quiet, internal crisis regarding who will inherit NPC's title, duty, or burden, complicated by sudden emotional shift, hidden disqualification, or clash of generations.
*   **Decay of Conviction:** Previously steadfast NPC begins to lose faith in their core motivation due to slow buildup of minor, unseen emotional papercuts (Doubt, Guilt, Exhaustion), leading to subtle self-sabotage or lethargy.
*   **Compartmentalization Failure:** NPC who strictly separates two aspects of their life (e.g., ruthless official vs. tender caretaker) finds boundary breaking down, causing uncharacteristic but humanizing behavior.
*   **Phantom Obligation:** NPC is bound by historical, deeply personal promise that directly contradicts their current realpolitik survival. They are discreetly looking for loophole.
*   **Projection of Grief/Anger:** NPC is dealing with profound, untouchable sorrow or rage they cannot express to true source. They actively project this onto secondary, safer target (minor faction, tradition, or PC).

### 4. CULTURAL & REALPOLITIK FRAMEWORKS (Societal Pressures & Dogma)
*   **Cost of Dogma (Mirror Subplot):** Strict cultural tradition or factional rule directly contradicts NPC's immediate, vital self-interest. NPC must quietly choose between immense personal loss and committing dangerous heresy.
*   **Heretical Sympathy (Contrast Subplot):** NPC or faction secretly begins adopting, protecting, or admiring practice, philosophy, or aesthetic belonging to rival or taboo culture.
*   **Prestige Trap:** NPC is forced to publicly uphold grand, costly lie or aggressive stance *solely* to prevent catastrophic loss of face, while secretly desperate for external force to give them low-profile "out."
*   **Institutional Rot:** NPC discovers that sacred system, guild, or religious pillar they rely on is functioning purely on momentum and is secretly hollow, corrupt, or bankrupt.
*   **Unintended Ripple (Hinged Subplot):** Seemingly insignificant, routine cultural or bureaucratic action taken by NPC weeks ago cascades into volatile friction or crisis that they must now discreetly manage.

### 5. BENEFICIAL RECIPROCITY FRAMEWORKS (Earned returns)
*(Triggered ONLY when positive reciprocity is mathematically earned per §8)*
*   **Unspoken Vouch:** PC experiences suddenly smoothed friction in world (bypassed bureaucracy, lowered prices, sudden access) because NPC quietly staked their own prestige on PC's behalf behind closed doors.
*   **Vulnerable Confession (Info Silo Break):** NPC shares piece of highly compartmentalized intel, personal history, or cultural lore, strictly as payment for past PC discretion or competence.
*   **Pragmatic Olive Branch:** NPC calculates that aligning with PC on minor, neutral issue yields long-term mutual benefit. They frame calculated political move as "personal favor" or gesture of (Warmth, Respect).
*   **Collateral Protection:** NPC subtly alters their own factional agenda to quietly shield PC from an incoming, unrelated AoE (Area of Effect) political or physical threat, without demanding immediate credit.
